WebBuddy - some tips.

Work with links in context.

Click on a link in an app such as Mail, Twitter or Evernote to open it without losing context.

[show-click-in-app]

Menu bar app.

WebBuddy is a menu bar app, so there is no dock icon.

You can configure browsers for WebBuddy by setting them in WebBuddy's preferences.

[img-pointing-to-menu-bar-icon] [img-preferences-screenshot]

Working with other browsers.

From WebBuddy, send a page to another browser using the buttons in the toolbar.

You can also click on a hyperlink in the app you're working with while holding down the ⌥(Option / Alt) or ⌥(Option / Alt) and ⇧(Shift) modifier keys, to open the link directly in a browser of your choice.

[show-modkey-click]

Navigation.

You can page back and forward in WebBuddy by swiping left or right.

[show-nav]
